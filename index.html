About CASMYR

CASMYR is a hybrid wholesale café and artisan pastry commissary serving both retail customers and business partners. We combine a premium café experience with a high-volume production facility, bridging the "Artisan Accessibility Gap" in Cavite.

Mission: To provide the local community with high-quality coffee and freshly made artisan pastries at affordable, competitive prices.
